Book Genres

Fantasy:

Fantasy is a genre that explores imaginative and supernatural elements within fictional worlds, often featuring magic, mythical creatures, and epic quests.

Examples

  1. Lord of the Rings
  2. Game of Thrones
  3. The Lion, The Witch and The Wardrobe
  4. The BFG
  5. The Golden Compass
