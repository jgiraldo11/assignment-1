Book Genres

Historical Fiction:

Historical fiction is a genre of literature that blends fictional characters and events with a historical setting, often aiming to recreate the time period's atmosphere, customs, and significant events. It provides readers with an immersive experience that combines elements of both history and storytelling, offering a unique perspective on the past while maintaining a foundation of factual accuracy.

Examples

  1. The Guernsey Literary and Potato Peel Pie Society
  2. The Book Thief
  3. To Kill a Mockingbird
  4. The Tattooist of Auschwitz
  5. All the Light We Cannot See
